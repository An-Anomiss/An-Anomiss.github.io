Andreea Simona Negrilă

Who am I?

A visual learner. Open. Positive. High-energy. Always looking to challenge my creative curiosity. I like to connect the dots and have a tendency to question everything. I‘m a problem solver, I strive to find the solution in any situation, always putting people and their needs at the center of my work.

My Background

I studied architecture and did a master's in Set and Costume Design. With over 10+ years of experience and 30+ successful projects, I refined my ability to devise creative solutions. I’m motivated and persuasive in managing, delegating and implementing tasks, aiming to inspire people in the project to trust and follow my vision.

My motivations

I work in a solution-oriented way to create a positive impact. My interest in the human condition is the driving force of my work. I do not hesitate to reposition myself in response to an ever-changing world. My aim is to get closer to the best version of myself, a position from where I can meaningfully give back.
