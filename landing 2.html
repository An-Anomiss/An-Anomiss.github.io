OTHER SPACES

Interior design for National Theatre in Craiova, Romania

- Theatre Ticket agency and non-conventional performing space - IA TE UITA?!
Supervised and actively participated in conversion of unused space into the new Ticket Agency plus a non-conventional performing space where regular meetings between actors and the public as well as other events such as book launches and press releases are taking place. The concept centred around the idea of chairs as characters.
- Chose chromatics and treatments for the floors, walls, and doors all around the Theatre Building thereby creating a new and fresh interior look in accordance with the theatre's image.
- Redesigned the General Manager's office adding a contemporary minimalistic touch aligned with his personality.
